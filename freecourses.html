Instructions for Certificate Generation

1. Click on the link of the course and click enroll.
2. Create your account with your email and mobile number, then provide the OTP (If it shows any error, come back and click on the link again).
3. Enter your email ID and password and click sign in.
4. Click on the three lines at the top right corner and go to "My Programs."
5. Click on the course you enrolled in.
6. Mark attendance for each day by clicking on "Mark Attendance."
7. After marking attendance for each day, refresh the page.
8. After refreshing the page, you will see the "Generate Certificate" option in green color at the top of the page.
9. Click on "Generate Certificate" and add your address by clicking on the edit option.
10. After filling the address, click enter and your certificate will be downloaded.
